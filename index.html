How To Play?

I have hidden a car behind one of these three doors.
Behind the other two doors there are goats.
If you choose the door with the car, you win the car!
If you choose a door with a goat, you get a goat.
Click on a door to choose it.
I will display one goat and allow you to stay with your choice or switch doors.
Choose your options and click keep or switch button.
I will then reveal the location of the car.
Click on play again button to restart.
Should you stay with your first choice, or switch?

You Win!

You Lose.
